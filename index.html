Welcome to the Melrose High School Ski/Board Club. Everyone is welcome to join at any time!

How to sign up:
  1. Contact one of our counselors, or club captains: Mr.Tucc, Mrs.Kane, Adam Weiss, Gavin Rowe, or Philip Dwyer for details on the next trip.
  2. Fill out the waiver supplied before the next trip.
  3. Keep up to date with costs and dates! Make sure to have all the equipment you may need, or pay extra for rentals.
